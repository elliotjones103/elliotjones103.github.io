Projects

  • MSc Dissertation - Martingale Defect in Asset Price Bubbles (79%)
    Developed continuous SDE and jump-driven strict local martingale models in Python to study asset price bubbles. Used Monte Carlo methods to generate option prices and applied option-implied diagnostics (e.g. put-call parity violations and the bubble gap) to detect and quantify bubble effects.
  • Monte Carlo Methods for Option Pricing (89%)
    Implemented Monte Carlo simulations with Euler-Maruyama schemes to price European options under Black-Scholes and Heston models. Investigated convergence by varying time steps and strikes, and used variance-reduction techniques to improve estimator accuracy.
  • Interest Rate Model Calibration (82%)
    Streamlit app that pulls option chains, cleans the surface and computes bubble-gap metrics under Black-Scholes, CEV and SABR-type models.
  • FX Derivatives Market Maker - Cardiff University (78%)
    Acted as an FX derivatives market maker, providing quotes, executing trades and hedging FX exposure using options. Analysed P&L decomposition and risk exposure using the Greeks and summarised performance in a final report.
  • US Equity Market Intraday Trader - Cardiff University (80%)
    Designed and backtested an intraday trading strategy for a US equity in Python using RSI-based indicators. Forward-tested the strategy via paper trading in TWS and evaluated performance using return and risk measures.
